Itero 5D-Scanner & NIRI - How to Use It?
NIRI stands for Near Infra-Red Imaging, which is a system used for early detection of enamel lesions. It offers higher sensitivity compared to bitewing radiography and has already demonstrated comparable sensitivity in detecting dentin lesions.
A multicenter clinical study has confirmed the benefits of this system as a tool for detecting and monitoring interproximal caries lesions above the gingiva without the need for X-ray radiation.
Journal of Dentistry (As of October 24, 2021)

Endodontic Billing: BEMA vs. GOZ Coverage
Endodontic procedures for patients with statutory health insurance are subject to cost-effectiveness requirements, similar to other areas of dental conservation. However, dental professional societies and university clinics are advocating for more therapeutic steps to enhance the quality and long-term outcomes of root canal treatment, based on research studies. Balancing compliance with statutory guidelines and meeting scientific standards can sometimes be challenging for practitioners, as these requirements often differ significantly.
